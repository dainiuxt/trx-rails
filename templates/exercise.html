TRX Chest Press

The TRX Chest Press is a bodyweight exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It's an effective alternative to traditional push-ups or bench presses.

Recommended Training

Sets

3

Reps

12

Rest

30 sec

Start Exercise

Instructions

  1. Stand facing away from the anchor point, holding the handles with arms extended at chest height.
  2. Lean forward, keeping your body straight from head to heels.
  3. Bend your elbows to lower your chest between the handles.
  4. Press back to the starting position by extending your arms.
  5. Keep your core engaged and maintain a straight body position throughout the movement.

Tips

  • To increase difficulty, move your feet closer to the anchor point.
  • To decrease difficulty, move your feet further from the anchor point.
  • Keep your elbows at a 45-degree angle to your body to protect your shoulders.
  • Don't let your hips sag or pike during the movement.

Variations

  • Single-arm TRX Chest Press
  • TRX Chest Press with Rotation
  • TRX Chest Press with Feet Elevated
