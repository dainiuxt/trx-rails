How to Use the Timer

1. Set Your Intervals

Adjust the work and rest times using the sliders. Work intervals are when you perform the exercise, and rest intervals are your recovery periods between sets.

2. Configure Sets and Rounds

Sets are repetitions of a single exercise. Rounds are complete cycles of all exercises in your workout. Adjust these based on your workout plan.

3. Start Your Workout

Press the play button to begin. The timer will guide you through your work and rest intervals, keeping track of your sets and rounds automatically.

4. Audio Cues

The timer provides audio signals when transitioning between work and rest periods. You can toggle sound on/off using the sound button.
